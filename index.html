About Me
Hello and thank you for visiting my webpage; allow me to introduce myself and share my background.
My name is Rory Mulcahey. I am a long-time computer nerd and have a passion for understanding how things work. I began my early career with a focus on the physical world of mechanical engineering. Then, I started programming journey through my curiosity for the subject, and learned initially through self-taught practices.
